FAQ

Common questions

Can one account switch roles later?

Roles are assigned during registration. If your role changes, an admin can create/update your access profile.

Is payment tracking available for both hall and meal dues?

Yes. The platform tracks hall fees and meal-related pending payments in a unified payment flow.

How do complaint and maintenance updates appear?

Updates appear in each role dashboard and Alerts Center, so users can follow status changes without external tools.
